The History of Beirul

The world of Beirul contains two large continent separated by vast oceans. The two large continents of Beirul are Bralea and Cahuts. Bralea is where the elves and humans originated from, but many thousands of years ago, elves decided to sail into the ocean, where they were they were never seen again by the humans of Bralea. The elves, found a continent, only inhabited by orc clans on the south west. These elves, decided to settle in the south eastern part of the continent, right above the Snow Archipelago, a sub continent of Cahuts.

The continent of Cahut is located in the southern hemisphere, with the northern part of the continent being a tropical environment with a lot swamps (like florida).

Time past and humans assimilated into cities, creating nations and becoming civilized. Due to this process, the majority of humans abandon most of their tribal costumes, practices and rituals while adopting higher and more noble gods. Therefore the practice of necromancy was frowned upon and became persecuted. Necromancers were hunted and armies rallied to defeat liches and their followers as well as their undead hoards. Due to all of these issues only the most powerful and secretive practitioners of these dark arts managed to survived and escaped into the ocean, searching for a new land. They found their new home in the northern part of Cahuts. Their they made their homes among the swamps and the the coastline, that came to be known as "The Dead Coast." Their society is ruled by a powerful lich king who goes by the title of the "Great Lich King."

852 years after the last practitioner of necromancy was killed on Bralea, there had been no reports of necromancy, to the point that a great part of the human population started beliving that necromancer never actualy existed and that they had just been inventions of the mind and excuses to acuse a fellow townsfolk and have them executed for revenge. At this time there had been a boom, a revolution in the sciences, technologies and magical arts. This also created a new age of exploration and adventuring, were united under a single empire, the humans of western Bralea adventured into the ocean, only to find the southern continent of Cahuts, There the humans found that elves still existed and where necromancers had gone. They also created colonies on the center coast of Cahuts. There they build the city of Coralia, their first city in this "new land."

100 years later, the expansion continued and the colonies expanded. After some mild wars and conflict for the first 30 years since the creation of Coralia, the different groups are starting to get allong, but still relationships are still rocky. To consolidate the relationships between the elves, orcs, humans and the necromancers. A treaty was announced to be signed in Coralia. For this reason a great festival was created. Today, there is only one week left until the treaty is signed and people from all over the lands are coming to celebrate.
