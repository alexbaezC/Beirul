Politics in Beirul

The Guild of Coralia

The large city of Coralia, is an economic powerhouse, due to being the largest human city in the entire continent. This has attracted many powerful merchants and other influencial individuals. Each one of those guilds controle an activity and sector of the city. All of the guilds leaders form part of the Coralia Common Allience which meets with the noble and Churches representatives. This meetings, that occure normaly once in the year, are called the Generalis.

The 6 Necromancer Clans

The Great Lich King rules over 6 great clans of necromacers. Each one of those clans is lead by a representative chosen by their king to be able to rule it. The clans, have a sort of rivalry and are only kept in check by their common devotion to the Great Lich King. To maintain their tensions to a minimum the King has made it so that each 10 years, one member of the chiefs family will go live in another tribe and vise versa.
